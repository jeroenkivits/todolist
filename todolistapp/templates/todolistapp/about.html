About this site:

This site is made for the 2id60 Web technology course of the TU Eindhoven.
On this site I provided a "to-do-list" generator application, which can be
used for making to do lists.

The to do list itself has several functionalities:
  • You can add a "to-do" to the list by pressing the light "+"-button in the
    top-right corner. This will take you to a page where you can enter your to-do.
  • When you made a mistake and want to edit a task you can go to the "to-do"
    page and click on that task. When you are logged in a button with a pencil
    on it will appear. If you click that button you can edit your to-do.
  • When you click on a task-name in the list on the to-do-list page,
    a new window will appear which shows only that task so you can read
    all available information over that to-do.

When planning a task/activity it is nice to know the weather, and therefore there
is a button called "Weather" which shows the weatherforecast for the coming days.
The weather forecast is provided by meteoblue.
One can also see the current date and time in the gray container.
