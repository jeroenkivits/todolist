About this site:

This site is made for the 2id60 Web technology course of the TU Eindhoven.
On this site I provided a "to-do-list" generator application, which can be
used for making to do lists.

The to do list itself has several functionalities:
  • You can add a "to-do" to the list by typing tekst in the white field saying
    "Enter task." and pressing the light blue "ADD"-button.
  • When a task is done you can click on it and it will become striked-trough
    and light-blue coloured, and a check-mark will appear before the task.
    When you click again on that task the checkmark & strikethrough will disappear.
  • You can also delete an added task by pressing the small cross on the right-
    end of the task.

When planning a task/activity it is nice to know the weather, and therefore there
is a button called "Weather" which shows the weatherforecast for the coming days.
The weather forecast is provided by meteoblue.
One can also see the current date and time in the gray container.
